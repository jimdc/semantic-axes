What you’re actually seeing. These axes are the learned associations of a word-embedding trained on web text — a mirror of how words co-occur, not a statement of how the world is. “father ↔ man” here is the same signal the bias-in-embeddings literature studies (Bolukbasi 2016, WEAT). Read it as the model’s associations, with all their stereotypes intact.

Static substrate: GloVe 6B (Wikipedia + Gigaword, 300-d), centered → L2-normalized → int8. SAE substrate: live feature activations via Neuronpedia. Axis method: difference-of-anchors (Bolukbasi 2016 / SemAxis 2018). Free & open source.
